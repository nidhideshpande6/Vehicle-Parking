About Software

The purpose of the project is to build an application program to reduce the manual work for managing the customers, types, parking slots, duration. It traces all the details.

1.Provides the searching facilities based on the various factors such as customers, duration, parking fees, vehicles.

2.Manages the information of parking fees.

3.Integrate all the records of the vehicle.

4.Editing, adding and updating of record is improved which results in proper resource management of customer data

The main objective of parking system is to manage the details of the customer, Duration, Parking fees, Vehicles.The project is built at the administrative end and thus only the administrator is guaranteed the access.

The smart parking system is considered beneficial for the car park operators, car park patrons as well as in environment conservation (Shaheen et al., 2005; Chinrungrueng et al., 2007). For the car park operators, the information gathered via the implementation of the Smart Parking System can be exploited to predict future parking patterns.

Pricing strategies can also be manipulated according to the information obtained to increase the company’s profit. In terms of environment conservation, the level of pollution can be reduced by decreasing vehicle emission (air pollutant) in the air (Shaheen et al., 2005). This can be attributed to the fact that vehicle travel is reduced. As fuel consumption is directly related to vehicle miles travelled, it will be reduces as well.
